ABOUT OUR UNIT

The Bundeswehr Roleplay group is a disciplined military simulation community on Roblox, dedicated to providing an authentic German armed forces experience.

Founded in 2023, we've grown to become one of the most respected military RP groups with regular trainings, operations, and events.

  • 100+ Active Members
  • Weekly Operations
  • Training Courses
  • Rank Progression
